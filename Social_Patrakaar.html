About us

We are a new Hindi News Digital Channel. Our focus is on sharing news and stories of socially vulnerable people. We want to show that media is not just for talking about political issues, but also for common citizens and their issues. The happiest people we know are those who lose themselves in the service of others. Social awareness and Education is the purpose of this channel. Educate yourself on important things which matter in your life and learn how you can make the world a better place for yourself and for everyone else. If you want a change in society.
